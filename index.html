Ziqiao Zhou
Senior Researcher at MSR Redmond

C.V. LinkedIn



I got my Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2020, where I was fortunate to be working with Professor Michael Reiter. My PhD thesis focuses on applying advanced information analysis technique to real software and hardware implementations. Before my PhD study, I obtained my B.S. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research interests are System Security and Computer-Aided Program Verification.
